# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Times

### Domestic Shipping (US)
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
- Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days
- Express Shipping: 1-2 business days

### International Shipping
- Canada/Mexico: 7-14 business days
- Europe: 10-21 business days
- Asia/Pacific: 14-28 business days
- Rest of World: 14-35 business days

## Shipping Costs

### Free Shipping
- Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping (domestic only)
- Applied automatically at checkout

### Standard Rates
- Under $25: $5.99
- $25-$49.99: $7.99
- International: Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same day
- Orders after 2 PM EST ship next business day
- No shipping on weekends or holidays

## Tracking
- Tracking number provided within 24 hours of shipment
- Track your order at supportflow.com/track
- Email notifications for shipping status updates

## Shipping Issues

### Lost Packages
- Contact us if package not received within expected timeframe
- We'll file a claim with the carrier
- Replacement or refund issued after claim resolution

### Damaged Packages
- Inspect package upon delivery
- Report damage within 48 hours with photos
- We'll arrange replacement or refund

### Wrong Address
- Ensure shipping address is correct before ordering
- Address changes possible within 1 hour of order
- Customer responsible for costs if package returned due to wrong address

## International Orders
- Customs duties and taxes are customer's responsibility
- Delays possible due to customs processing
- We cannot mark packages as "gift" to avoid duties
