# Account Help & FAQ

## Creating an Account

### Registration
- Visit supportflow.com/signup
- Provide email address and create password
- Verify email (check spam folder if not received)
- Complete profile with shipping information

### Benefits
- Track orders in real-time
- Save multiple shipping addresses
- View order history
- Faster checkout
- Exclusive member discounts

## Login Issues

### Forgot Password
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create new password (min 8 characters)

### Account Locked
- Locked after 5 failed login attempts
- Wait 30 minutes or use "Forgot Password"
- Contact support if still unable to access

### Email Not Recognized
- Ensure you're using the correct email
- Check if you created account with social login (Google, Facebook)
- Contact support to verify account status

## Managing Your Account

### Update Email Address
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Profile
3. Enter new email address
4. Verify new email

### Change Password
1. Account Settings > Security
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password twice
4. Save changes

### Update Payment Methods
- Add/remove credit cards in Payment Methods section
- We don't store full card numbers (PCI compliant)
- Expired cards automatically removed

### Shipping Addresses
- Save up to 10 addresses
- Set a default address
- Edit or delete addresses anytime

## Order History

### View Orders
- Access complete order history in "My Orders"
- Filter by date, status, or product
- Download invoices for tax purposes

### Reorder
- One-click reorder from order history
- Automatically uses last shipping address
- Review before confirming

## Privacy & Security

### Data Protection
- We use industry-standard encryption
- Never share personal data with third parties
- GDPR and CCPA compliant

### Delete Account
- Request account deletion in Settings
- Data removed within 30 days
- Cannot be undone
- Active orders must be completed first

## Newsletter & Communications

### Unsubscribe
- Click "Unsubscribe" at bottom of any email
- Manage preferences in Account Settings
- Note: Transactional emails (order updates) cannot be disabled
