# Product Warranty Information

## Warranty Coverage

### Standard Warranty
All products include a manufacturer's warranty:
- Electronics: 1 year limited warranty
- Accessories: 90 days warranty
- Furniture: 1 year warranty
- Appliances: 1-2 years (varies by product)

## What's Covered

### Manufacturing Defects
- Defective materials or workmanship
- Parts failure under normal use
- Functionality issues not caused by user

### Examples
- Product stops working without cause
- Buttons or controls malfunction
- Screen/display defects
- Battery issues (if not physically damaged)

## What's NOT Covered

### Exclusions
- Normal wear and tear
- Accidental damage or drops
- Water/liquid damage
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect function
- Lost or stolen items

### Benefits

## Claiming Warranty

### Process
1. Contact support with order number and issue description
2. Provide photos/videos of the defect
3. Our team reviews within 24-48 hours
4. Receive repair, replacement, or refund

### Timeline
- Review: 1-2 business days
- Shipping label provided if return needed
- Replacement shipped within 3-5 business days of receiving defective item
