# Refund Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and straightforward.

## Refund Eligibility

### Time Window
- Standard products: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 30 days with original packaging
- Opened software: Not eligible for refund
- Custom/personalized items: Not eligible for refund

### Conditions
1. Item must be in original condition
2. All accessories and packaging must be included
3. Proof of purchase required
4. Product must not be damaged by customer

## Refund Process

1. **Contact Support**: Reach out to our support team with your order number
2. **Review**: We'll review your request within 24 hours
3. **Approval**: If approved, you'll receive return shipping instructions
4. **Return**: Ship the item back using provided label
5. **Processing**: Refund processed within 5-10 business days after we receive the item

## Refund Methods
- Original payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- Store credit (issued immediately upon approval)

## Shipping Costs
- We cover return shipping for defective items
- Customer responsible for return shipping on buyer's remorse

## Exceptions
- Sale items marked "Final Sale" are not refundable
- Gift cards are non-refundable
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless item was defective

## Contact
For refund questions, email support@supportflow.com or submit a ticket.
